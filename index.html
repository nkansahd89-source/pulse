How bias is computed

  1. Pull this week's economic calendar (actual vs. forecast for CPI, GDP, jobs, rate decisions) for all 8 currencies.
  2. Pull live headlines from the forex news wires listed on the left.
  3. Score each currency: calendar beats/misses (weighted by impact) plus keyword sentiment in headlines, mapped to central banks and countries.
  4. Every pair's bias is just the gap between its two currencies' scores. Indices, metals, oil, and crypto get their own keyword read.

This is a rule-based estimate, not a broker's live order book — treat it as a fundamentals summary to speed up your own read, not a signal to trade blindly.
